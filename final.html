Project Description

This coffee table is for anyone missing a warm and cozy atmosphere in their home. As an excellent addition to every room, it casts wonderful organic shapes on its top surface and if the conditions are right, even onto the room's walls. It reaches this by a light beam hitting two rotating Mylar sheet-coated surfaces.

In addition, all internal surfaces of the box are Mylar coated, breaking the light multiple times before it hits the top surface. The box's classy appearance is shaped by the materials it's made of; wood, glass, and paper. We chose these materials very consciously, to add further comfort to the room.
